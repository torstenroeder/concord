Person Concordancer 1.0

This is a tool to find general identifiers for persons, just by providing some basic data. This is version 1.0.

Parameters

You can submit a request by a combination of URL parameters.

  • n (principal name)
  • on (other names)
  • dob (date of birth, ISO 8601)
  • pob (place of birth)
  • dod (date of death, ISO 8601)
  • pod (place of death)
  • desc (description)

examples:

  • http://pdrdev.bbaw.de/concord/?on=Sebastian&n=Bach&dob=1685&dod=1750&pob=Eisenach&desc=Organist
  • http://pdrdev.bbaw.de/concord/?n=Virchow&desc=Arzt

Results

Results will be returned in XML.

If there are more than 5 results, the following 5 results will be only displayed with a score of 40 or more. More than 10 results will not be displayed.

Scores

  • Concordance of name and other names = 40
  • Concordance of name and year of birth/death = 30
  • Concordance of exact date and place of birth = 15
  • Concordance of exact date and place of death = 15
  • Concordance of name and description = 20

How does this work?

The tool depends on the resource at http://toolserver.org/~apper/pd/. It is recommended to check the availability before using the concordancer.
